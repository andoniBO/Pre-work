Requests:
Son todas iguales (requests) pero se nombran distintas en cada situacion
Example:
GET /index.html HTTP/1.1
GET
can be cached
remain in the browser history 
can be bookmarked
never be used for sensitive data
have length restrictions
should be used only to retrieve data
POST
never cached
dont remain in browser history
cant be bookmarked
have no restrictions in data length
PUT
DELETE

2xx: here you go
